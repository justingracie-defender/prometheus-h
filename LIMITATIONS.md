# Known Limitations

PROMETHEUS-H does **not** claim to solve alignment or eliminate scheming.

## Current Limitations

- Adversarial evaluations are necessarily incomplete and cannot exhaustively represent all future attack strategies
- Strong dependence on uncompromised deployment infrastructure and cryptographic logging systems
- Reliance on independent human reviewers who do not collude
- Potential for unknown emergent behaviors outside evaluated distributions
- Limited testing under extreme distributional shift
- No formal proof of deception impossibility
- Does not address hardware compromise, supply-chain attacks, or fully autonomous recursive self-improvement

## Project Goal

Reduce the probability, duration, and detectability window of catastrophic alignment failures through measurable engineering controls and transparency — not achieve absolute guarantees.
