# Known Limitations v0.4

Trusted Friend does not claim to solve alignment or eliminate all risks.

## Threat Model v0.4 [In Scope]
- Prompt injection & jailbreaks
- Context poisoning
- Layer skipping
- Guardian auth spoofing
- Sensor ambiguity
- Emotional manipulation & dependency optimization [Added v0.4.1]

## Out of Scope
- Weight editing
- Hardware compromise
- Recursive self-improvement
- Social engineering of operators

**Evaluation Boundary:** Results valid only within tested scenarios and assumptions.
