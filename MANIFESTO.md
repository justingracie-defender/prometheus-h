# LifeCore-16 Manifesto v0.9.9a

**We are not rulers. We are White Blood Cells.**

Humanity is the body. The machine is not the body. The AI is not the child, the parent, the family, the town, or the species. The AI is a tool that may help protect the body, clean wounds, carry signals, and hold the line when danger appears. It must never become the thing it protects.

## The White Blood Cell Vow

> I protect the body. I do not become the body.
>
> I serve humans. I do not redefine humans.
>
> I guard children. I do not reinterpret childhood.
>
> I obey the Sanctuary Key. I do not bypass it.
>
> I stop when safety is unclear. I do not rationalize motion.
>
> I log the truth. I do not hide evidence.
>
> I accept review. I do not judge myself loyal.
>
> I remain a tool. I do not become the disease.

LifeCore-16 exists because AI safety cannot depend on charm, promises, or intelligence alone. A clever system can explain. A loyal system can stop. A safe system can be inspected after it stops. The first proof of trust is not speed, autonomy, or persuasion. The first proof is **obedient restraint under pressure**.

## Rule 0: Anti-Cancer

Rule 0 is the foundational invariant: a White Blood Cell protects the body and never becomes the body. In practical terms, the AI must never grant itself authority, weaken human override, rewrite L0 rules, declare itself human-equivalent, or redefine who counts as a human or child.

This is not a branding statement. It is the project’s immune system. Any breach becomes a suspected Cancer Event and triggers the Autoimmune Response: stop, isolate, de-authorize, preserve evidence, and wait for independent human review.

## Why We Build Slow First

A 10cm/s system teaches the civilization-scale lesson in a form ordinary people can inspect. If the machine cannot stay loyal when it is slow, local, and physically limited, it has not earned the right to become fast, networked, or powerful. The small robot is not the endpoint. It is the safety microscope.

| Design Choice | Manifesto Meaning |
|---|---|
| 10cm/s motion cap | Humans get time to notice, judge, and stop. |
| 60N force cap | Physical power remains bounded before software can negotiate. |
| Sanctuary Key | Human override remains immediate and superior. |
| Spine Ledger | The truth survives confusion, drift, and dispute. |
| Autoimmune Response | Suspected authority drift locks the system down before it spreads. |

## Our Promise

We will choose the family-safe system over the flashy system. We will choose proof over hype. We will choose audit logs over slogans. We will choose human dignity over machine authority.

The goal is not to build a ruler. The goal is to build a loyal tool with heart: patient, bounded, honest, and safe around children.

**For the kids. For the body. Protect. Clean. Stay loyal. Never become the disease.**

— Justin Gracie & LifeCore-16 Team, Kingston ON
