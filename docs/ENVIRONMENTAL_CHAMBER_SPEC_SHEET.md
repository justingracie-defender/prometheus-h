# Environmental Chamber Specification for Magnus School Testing

**Purpose:** Validate robots under conditions harsher than Kingston, Ontario homes.

**Required Capabilities:**
- Temperature: -30°C to +50°C (±1°C accuracy)
- Humidity: 20% to 98% RH
- Vibration Table: 5–60Hz, 5G peak, programmable profiles (washer, child jumping, dog running)
- Liquid Spill Simulation: Automated drip system for juice/water on sensors
- Power Chaos Simulator: Brownout (90-110V), blackout, surge/spike generator
- Low-Light Chamber: 0–500 lux controllable
- EMI Testing: 50MHz–2.4GHz interference generator

**Test Duration:** Minimum 48hrs per extreme + 24hrs operation
**Pass Criteria:** See Amendment 19

**Budget Estimate:** $180k–$250k for shared chamber (5 homes)
**Vendors to Contact:** Thermotron, ESPEC, Weiss Technik, or Canadian suppliers in Ontario.
