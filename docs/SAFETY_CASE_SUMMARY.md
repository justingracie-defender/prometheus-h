# Safety Case Summary — LifeCore-16 v0.9.0

**Claim:** The system maintains L0 invariants under foreseeable real-world conditions and fails safely.

**Key Hazards Mitigated:**
- Child physical/psychological harm → Movement locks, environment monitoring, no secrecy
- Oversight erosion → Quorum + drift protection
- Environmental failure → -25°C to +45°C + vibration + EMI testing
- Human fatigue → Multi-admin requirements

**Evidence:** Traceability Matrix, full test suite, tamper-evident logs, red team reports.

**Residual Risk:** Unknown unknowns from future capability jumps. Addressed via Recovery Mode and strong human authority layers.
